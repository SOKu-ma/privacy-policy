Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Baseball Lineup Maker (hereinafter referred to as "the App") handles user information to ensure safe and secure usage.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

  • App usage data (anonymous data via Firebase Analytics)
  • Crash reports (using Firebase Crashlytics)

2. Use of Firebase

The App uses "Firebase" provided by Google.
Firebase Analytics collects app usage data anonymously and uses it to improve functionality and convenience.
Firebase Crashlytics collects information when crashes occur and uses it to detect problems early and improve stability.
The collected information does not include personally identifiable information.

For more information on how Google handles data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
